Weapon Masteries 1.2F
- These are masteries that grant passive bonuses, Mastery levels are gained from killing mosters using certain weapon in each mastery group, there are 5 groups in total, Onslaught, Swiftness, Safeguard, Ranged and Mystical. Players can use @wi or @winfo to learn what bonuses the gain from their weapons.
- Offhand can recive 1/2 effect shields being considered Safeguard

Onslaught:	2HSword,1HAxe,2HAxe,1HMace,2HMace(unused),Knuckle
Swiftness:	Specialty,Dagger,Fuuma Shuriken,Katar
Safeguard:	Unarmmed,1HSword,1HSpear,2HSpear,Shield
Ranged:		Bow,Instrument,Whip,Revolver,Rifle,Gatling Gun,Shotgun,Grenade Launcher
Mystical:	Rod,Book,2HStaff,Scythe(custom weapon)
